About Us

Smart Resume Analyzer is a cutting-edge platform designed to help professionals understand their skills, optimize their resumes, and navigate their career paths with confidence.

Our advanced algorithms analyze your resume using industry-standard ATS (Applicant Tracking System) criteria, extracting key skills and providing personalized insights for career development.

We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to showcase their best self to employers. That's why we've built tools that bridge the gap between your qualifications and how they're presented.
